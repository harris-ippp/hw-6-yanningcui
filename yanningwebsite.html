Asian-Style Pork Chop

Step 1: In a medium bowl, mix together the eggs, milk, garlic, salt and pepper.

Step 2: Heat oil in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat.

Step 3: Rinse pork chops with water, then dip in the egg mixture.

Coat with panko crumbs, dip in the egg mixture again, then coat with another layer of panko crumbs.

Step 4: Fry for about 5 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

Ingredients including:

Ingredients
2 eggs 1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon milk 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
8 thin cut boneless pork chops 1 1/2 cups panko crumbs
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic salt to taste
